Just as the promise of technology in marketing is undeniable, so are the challenges facing many companies in integrating technology into their marketing. In many organizations, marketing isn't coming close to their potential in digital marketing, frequently due to technological obstacles, whether internal or external.

 

Know when to Bring in the Experts

Some companies try to build the perfect team interanally. You are not Amazon or a digital marketing agency or technology integrator. Bring experts to lead the charge, and make sure to integrate your team so you can support and maintain.

Embrace Digital Marketing

In recent years, the trends has been to call this digital marketing, but technonlogy and digital experiences are now so woven into daily marketing activities, there is really no true differentation.

Be Open, Not Closed

Use open source and open standards. Enbrace solutions which integrate versus closed solutions. Also talk about being open with customers and looking for feedback.

Good Enough Beats Perfect

A good thing which launches on time is better than the "perfect" solution which never launches.
