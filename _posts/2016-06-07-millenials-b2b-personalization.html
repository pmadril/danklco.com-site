According to the Pew Research Center, Millennials became the largest generation in the workforce Q1 2015. The rise of the Millennial Generation in the workspace will have a profound impact on company culture, expectations, and practices, but many organizations have failed to anticipate how this new generation will influence company's expectations for B2B commerce.
